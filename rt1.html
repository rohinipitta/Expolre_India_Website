Leonia Resort

Leonia Holistic Destination, located at Bommaraspet, Shamirpet, Ranga Reddy District, captivates with its unique offerings and integrated facilities. The resort is easily accessible, being just about a kilometer away from the outer ring road, towards the Karimnagar highway, ensuring a hassle-free journey for its guests.

For those seeking a blend of luxury and comfort, the accommodation options at Leonia are designed to cater to every preference. The Lagoon Villas offer a rustic yet elegant setting with a 99m long pool bay, while the Celestia Suites exude grandeur and luxury, equipped with modern amenities including video conferencing facilities. The Ohbiz Hotel, with its 14 stories, caters to business travelers, wedding guests, and individual travelers, offering a classic hospitality experience with its adjacent Biz lawn for exclusive events.

Culinary adventures at Leonia are diverse, with restaurants ranging from Sun Moon and Bistro to Shahi Handi, catering to a variety of palates from Mughal to European flavors. The Water Galaxy restaurant adds a unique dining experience with live music and performances, enriching your meals with memorable moments.

The resort boasts an array of activities and amenities, including Splash, a water park with rain dance, DJ music, and slides for both kids and adults and many other exciting activities.

Leonia also prides itself on its spa services at Juventa, where guests can indulge in over 200 wellness experiences, including aqua therapies, ancient Indian treatments, and beauty treatments, among others. The resort has been recognized with several awards, including the AsiaSpa award and World Luxury Spa award, for its exceptional spa and wellness offerings.

For those planning a day outing, Leonia offers a comprehensive package that includes access to various activities, meals, and the use of resort amenities. The package is thoughtfully designed to cater to guests looking for a refreshing escape and engaging activities, ensuring a memorable experience for everyone.

In conclusion, Leonia Holistic Destination stands as a beacon of tranquility and luxury, offering a perfect blend of leisure and adventure. Its strategic location, coupled with a wide range of amenities and activities, makes it an ideal choice for those looking to escape the mundane and immerse themselves in a holistic experience, making it a must-visit resort in Shamirpet for a day outing.
