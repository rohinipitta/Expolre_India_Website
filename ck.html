CK Birla Hospital

The CK Birla Hospital is a NABH accredited, multi-speciality hospital located in Gurgaon. It is a proud part of the $2.8 billion diversified CK Birla Group that has also been serving India’s healthcare needs for over 50 years.

The Gurgaon hospital is led by an experienced team of over 100 specialists. We are a proud partner to thousands of happy families & patients and continue to offer international standards of clinical care with a robust focus on safety, integrity and compassion. We rely on both international and national guidelines & protocols of healthcare delivery and bring world-class care closer to home.

Our state-of-the-art technology and facilities enable real-time communication and seamless collaboration across caregivers to ensure accuracy and the best possible results. We see our patients as our partners and cater to their diverse healthcare needs across all stages of life. Our designated ‘care team’ approach allows our patients to be actively involved in their clinical decision making and access multi-skilled caregivers for well-rounded advice and treatment.
