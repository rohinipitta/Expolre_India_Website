Khol Hi-Raitan Wildlife Sanctuary

It is located on the Siwalik foothills. It is contiguous to Rajaji National Park (Uttarakhand) and Simbalbara National Park (Himachal Pradesh). It is bounded by Yamuna River to the east. Vegetation: tropical moist deciduous forest. Major Fauna: Panthers, rusty-spotted cat, chital, goral, nilgai, porcupine, small Indian civet, rhesus macaque.
