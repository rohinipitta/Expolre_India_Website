Big Foot Museum

The Big Foot Museum is a theme park is South Goaâ€™s Loutolim village that depicts the rural life of Goa as it used to be about a hundred years ago. It was founded and run by the artist named Maendra Alavares.

What To Expect?

A great place for history buffs, the museum showcases a great collection of traditional artefacts and art and is situated in an open-air setting. Do explore the artworks done by children and renowned artists of state and National fame.

Tips

The timings are from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm. Loutolim is close to Margao so you can plan a combined trip.
