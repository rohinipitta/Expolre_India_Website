Kasauli

The ideal choice for travellers staying in or close to Haryana is Kasauli. It is one of the best hill stations close to Haryana and is a perfect spot for having a family vacation or a short trip with friends. The true splendour of nature will make you want to stay at this place. It also has a wide range of places to see and has diverse fauna and flora. Kasauli was established as a cantonment town in 1842 by the Britishers. If you are a person who loves trekking then you must visit this place. This place has a lot of trekking routes and you can enjoy beautiful sunsets and sunrises. Places to visit in Kasauli are Mall Road, Christ's Church, Sunset Point and Lover's Lane. The distance from Haryana is 288 km.
