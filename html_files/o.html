Dr.Olavo Ribeiro Hospital

Located in the heart of Mapusa, this hospital was established in 1939 by Dr. Olavo Ribeiro Sr., A leading obstetrician-gynaecologist of Goa.

It is now managed by Dr. Olavo Ribeiro Jr., a General Surgeon (M.S.) trained at Mumbai's prestigious King Edward Memorial Hospital (KEM) and Dr. R N Cooper General Hospital and an Ex-Lecturer of Goa Medical College.

With over 8 decades of exceptional service, this hospital perseveres to uphold a high ethical code with full transparency, efficiency, the best treatment options available while striving to maintain a high standard of hygiene and homely comfort.
