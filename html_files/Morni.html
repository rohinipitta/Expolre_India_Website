Morni Hills

A weekend escape to Morni Hills is ideal from Chandigarh and Delhi. It is a lesser known hill station so you must try to visit this place. The Shivalik range of the Himalayas serves as the town's backdrop, and it is encircled on all sides by dense greenery. The weather at this place is very pleasant and comfortable throughout the year. Places to see near Morni hills are Tikkar Taal, Thakurdwara temple and Morni Fort. The distance from Haryana is 265 km.
