Aster Hospital

A multispeciality hospital with the only integrated modern OT complex in GOA. Highly complex Laproscopic and Minimally invasive surgeries in Cancer, General Surgery, Obesity, Paediatrics, Gynecology, Orthopedics, Urology and Plastic with Cosmetic surgeries are carried out daily. We also have a sophisticated neurology department with MFU, ventilators and qualified neonatologists. We have well trained staff in all the rooms and the doctors are available when needed. Modern Dialysis units with full time nephrologists is also there.
