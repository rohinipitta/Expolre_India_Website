Looking for a place to stay in Visakhapatnam? Then look no further than APTDC Haritha Beach Resort, a family-friendly resort that brings the best of Visakhapatnam to your doorstep.
You’ll enjoy relaxing rooms that offer a flat screen TV and air conditioning, bringing comfort and convenience to your visit.
The resort features room service. Plus, APTDC Haritha Beach Resort offers free breakfast, providing a pleasant respite from your busy day. For guests with a vehicle, free parking is available.
If you’re looking for a seafood restaurant, consider a visit to Vihar, D Cabana, or Sea Inn - Raju Ka Dhaba, which are all conveniently located a short distance from Aptdc Haritha Beach Hotel.
If you are interested in exploring Visakhapatnam, check out one of the parks, such as Kailasagiri Park, Vuda City Central Park, and Tenneti Park.
We’re sure you’ll enjoy your stay at APTDC Haritha Beach Resort as you experience everything Visakhapatnam has to offer.
